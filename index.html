Our Top Selling Dishes

Taste the Authentic Flavors of the Mediterranean

01

Chopped Salad with Oregano Vinaigrette

Our Chopped Salad with Oregano Vinaigrette is a fresh and zesty mix of chopped vegetables, including crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and tangy red onions, tossed together with a flavorful oregano vinaigrette. It's a perfect light and refreshing option for a side dish or a quick lunch.

02

Chicken Shawarma Salad with Lemon Tahini Dressing

Our Chicken Shawarma Salad with Lemon Tahini Dressing is a mouthwatering blend of marinated grilled chicken, crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and cucumber slices, topped with a creamy and tangy lemon tahini dressing. It's a satisfying and healthy meal that's perfect for lunch or dinner.

03

Mediterranean Hummus Bowl

Our Mediterranean Hummus Bowl is a delicious and healthy option that's packed with flavor and nutrients. It's made with creamy hummus, crisp lettuce, juicy cherry tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and tangy pickled onions, all drizzled with a flavorful olive oil and lemon juice dressing. This dish is a perfect option for a light and satisfying lunch or dinner.
